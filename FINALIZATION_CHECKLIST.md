# Finalization Checklist

Before clicking Finalize Round, confirm:

- All active players have 18 hole scores, or are marked as withdrawn/DNF.
- Every group has been reviewed by its scorekeeper.
- Skins screen shows final skin winners/no-skin holes.
- Places payout screen matches the number of players.
- Ties have been split and rounded down correctly.
- Quota-change preview has been reviewed.
- Backup/export has been saved.

After finalization:

- Place winnings are posted.
- Skins winnings are posted.
- Season money list is updated.
- Quotas are updated for the next round.
- Scores are locked unless admin unlocks them.
