# Bear Tracker Go-Live Checklist

## Before the first Saturday test

1. Confirm the latest files are committed and pushed to GitHub.
2. Open the GitHub Pages URL on a desktop and iPhone.
3. Confirm the app loads from the Home Screen icon.
4. Export a backup from the app before testing.
5. Create a test round with 6-8 players.
6. Create two groups and assign one scorekeeper per group.
7. Enter scores for three holes.
8. Review leaderboard, quota +/-, net skins, and payout preview.
9. Finalize the test round.
10. Confirm quotas and money totals update as expected.
11. Restore from the pre-test backup if needed.

## First real Saturday recommendation

Use Bear Tracker in parallel with the paper card for one round. Treat the paper card as the official backup until everyone is comfortable.
