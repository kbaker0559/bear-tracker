# Bear Tracker Admin Operating Manual

## Saturday Morning
1. Open Bear Tracker.
2. Confirm the round date, course, and active players.
3. Add guests if needed.
4. Update any known handicap or quota changes.
5. Drag players into groups.
6. Assign one scorekeeper per group.
7. Publish the round.

## During Play
- Scorekeepers enter gross scores for their group.
- The app calculates net scores, Stableford points, quota +/- and skins.
- Admin can correct scores if needed.

## After Play
1. Review missing scores.
2. Review leaderboard and skins.
3. Review payout preview.
4. Confirm tie splits.
5. Finalize the round.
6. Export/backup the results.
7. Save the copied results summary for email/text distribution.

## Correction Rule
Before finalization, admin corrections update results immediately. After finalization, corrections should be treated as an adjustment and logged.
