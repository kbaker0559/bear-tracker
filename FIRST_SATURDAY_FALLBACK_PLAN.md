# First Saturday Fallback Plan

For the first real round, use Bear Tracker and the paper card together.

## If someone has trouble

- The group scorer enters that player’s scores.
- Admin can correct scores before finalization.
- Paper scorecard remains the backup record.

## If phones or internet fail

- Continue on paper.
- Enter scores into Bear Tracker after the round.
- Export a backup after finalizing.

## After the round

Compare Bear Tracker results to the spreadsheet/paper method before paying out.
