# Bear Tracker Scorekeeper Guide

## Before the round
1. Open Bear Tracker.
2. Enter your PIN.
3. Choose your group.
4. Confirm the players in your group are correct.

## During the round
1. After each hole, open the group score screen.
2. Enter the gross score for each player in your group.
3. Tap Save Hole.
4. Confirm the app moves to the next hole.

## Important notes
- Enter gross scores only.
- The app calculates net, Stableford points, quota +/-, and skins.
- If a score is wrong, correct it before the round is finalized.
- The admin can correct any score.

## After the round
- Make sure all 18 holes are entered for every player in your group.
- Tell the admin when your group is complete.
