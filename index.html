Core Rules Implemented

AreaRule
StablefordDouble bogey or worse 0, bogey 1, par 2, birdie 4, eagle 6, double eagle 8.
LeaderboardSorted by Stableford points minus quota.
SkinsNet only. Lowest unique net score wins. Ties cancel. No carryovers.
Places9–15 players pay 4 places. 16+ players pay 5 places.
TiesTied players split the place money involved, rounded down to the nearest dollar.
Quota increasesOnly players winning place money can increase quota.
Quota decreasesPlayers below quota decrease according to the quota table.

v1.4 Saturday Test + Score Audit

This release adds a practical testing checklist before live group use.

  • Verify scorekeepers can enter scores for their group.
  • Test quota +/- and Stableford scoring with sample holes.
  • Test skins with both a unique low net and a tied low net.
  • Export a backup before and after a test round.

Next technical milestone: connect these workflows to Supabase real-time tables.

Score Correction & Audit Flow

v1.5 adds the rules for safe score corrections before and after finalization.

  • Scorekeepers can correct their own group before finalization.
  • Admin can correct any score.
  • After finalization, corrections require admin unlock.
  • Every change should record old score, new score, editor, time, and reason.

Finalize Round Checklist
