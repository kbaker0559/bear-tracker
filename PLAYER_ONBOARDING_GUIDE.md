# Player Onboarding Guide

## What Players Need to Know
- Open Bear Tracker from the shared link.
- Add it to the phone Home Screen if desired.
- Use your 4-digit PIN.
- If you are not a scorekeeper, you can still view leaderboard, skins and results.
- If you are a scorekeeper, enter gross scores for your entire group.

## Scorekeeper Instructions
1. Open Group Scoring.
2. Confirm the group is correct.
3. Select the current hole.
4. Tap each player's gross score.
5. Tap Save Hole.
6. Continue to the next hole.

## Important
Enter gross score only. The app handles net, Stableford, quota and skins.
