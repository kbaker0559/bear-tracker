# Troubleshooting Guide

## App does not load
- Refresh the page.
- Confirm cellular signal or Wi-Fi.
- Try opening the link directly in Safari or Chrome.

## Score not showing on leaderboard
- Confirm the scorekeeper tapped Save Hole.
- Check the player is active in the round.
- Check the score was entered on the correct hole.

## Wrong net score
- Confirm player handicap.
- Confirm hole handicap/stroke index.
- Confirm par for the hole.

## Wrong quota +/-
- Confirm player quota.
- Confirm all hole scores are entered.
- Check Stableford points by hole.

## Wrong skins result
- Net skins only.
- Lowest unique net score wins.
- Ties cancel the skin.
- No carryovers.

## GitHub update not appearing
- Wait 1-3 minutes after Push origin.
- Refresh browser.
- On iPhone, close/reopen the Home Screen app.
